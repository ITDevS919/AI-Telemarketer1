# 5 Steps Marketing™ -- Updated Structured Pitch

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# STEP 1 -- INTRODUCTION

## A -- Gatekeeper: Reach Decision Maker

Hi, can I speak to the owner please?\
(It's Jay.)\
(It's nothing serious.)\
It's just a quick introductory call.

If unavailable → move on.\
If available → continue.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## B -- Relax the Prospect

Hi ... It's nothing serious.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## C -- Reason for Contact

It's just a quick introductory call.\
I wanted to give you the chance to see some information on how to reduce
costs in your business.

My name's Jay from Proactiv.\
Sorry, who am I speaking with?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## D -- CREATE INTEREST (CRITICAL CHECKPOINT)

Proactiv have been established since 2009.\
We've developed several unique concepts that help businesses:

• Reduce overheads\
• Increase word-of-mouth referrals\
• Eliminate advertising costs

It's genuinely a game-changer.

### PRE-CLOSE (Checkpoint #1)

That sounds pretty interesting, I'm sure you'd agree?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### IF POSITIVE:

Move to Close (Step 4 -- Explain How We Work)

### IF "NOT INTERESTED":

I completely appreciate that --- I wouldn't expect you to be overly
interested from such a brief introduction.

Let me just ask you something quickly ---\
Every business likes saving money, right?

If still negative: All I'm really looking to do is send you some
information by email about reducing overheads.\
Would you be open to receiving that?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### IF "WHAT IS IT?" / NEEDS MORE INFO:

I understand --- I've been quite brief.

All I'm looking to do is quickly run the information by you and, if you
like the sound of it, give you the chance to see some FREE samples. We
don't send junk mail.

I've just got a couple of quick questions so I can see which concept is
most relevant.

Move to Step 2.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# STEP 2 -- PRESENTATION (QUALIFY + PROBLEM)

## E -- Qualify

Could you handle more customers in your business?

## F -- Highlight Problem

Government statistics state 25.3% of MOTs are carried out late in the
UK.

## G -- Fact Find

Do you currently have anything in place to help customers remember their
MOT due date?

I'm sure you'd agree word-of-mouth is the best way to attract new
customers?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# STEP 3 -- EXPLANATION (STORY SELLING + MICRO PRE-CLOSES)

Rule: After each story → PRE-CLOSE\
If positive → move toward close\
If neutral → continue\
If negative → redirect or email exit

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story 1 -- Why Cards Are KEPT

Plastic cards look and feel like credit cards.\
They are durable, perceived as valuable, and kept in wallets.

### Pre-Close

They sound like a good idea, I'm sure you'd agree?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story 2 -- Referral Tracking System

Cards get passed on.\
You can monitor referrals and reward customers.

### Pre-Close

It sounds like a strong concept, doesn't it?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story 3 -- Key Fob Comparison

Solid plastic fobs last 5--6 years versus laminated versions that peel.

Makes sense why solid lasts longer, right?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story 4 -- Writable MOT Reminder

Writable coating allows MOT due date reminders on customer keys.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# STEP 4 -- CLOSE

## Master Pre-Close

Overall, it sounds like a pretty solid idea for a garage like yours,
yeah?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Explain How We Work

We show business owners samples over camera.\
10--15 minutes.\
No travel required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Assumptive Close

I've got availability later today or tomorrow.\
Which works better for you?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# STEP 5 -- CONSOLIDATION

## Confirm Decision Makers

Is anyone else involved in making this decision?

## Confirm Contact Details

Verify spelling, business name, mobile (WhatsApp), and email.

## Prepare for Next Stage

Customer receives: • Email\
• WhatsApp confirmation\
• Appointment reminder\
• Link with testimonials and offers

## Farewell

It's been great speaking with you.\
We look forward to speaking tomorrow at 6:30pm.\
Enjoy the rest of your day.
